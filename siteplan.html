Paws & Whiskers Shelter – Site Plan

Site Name

Paws & Whiskers Shelter

This title was utilized since it's friendly, easy to remember, and best illustrates the fact that the site deals with helping both cats and dogs. It is attractive to pet owners and simple to brand.

Optional domain availability: pawswhiskershelter.org

Site Purpose

The site gives information about the shelter, shows pets ready for adoption, and offers information on how people can help by volunteering, donating, or fostering. The goal is to find homes for pets and involve the public in animal welfare.

Scenarios

  • How do I view pets that are currently up for adoption?
  • How do I become a foster or volunteer parent?
  • How can I donate in order to help the shelter?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #4A90E2 (Blue) – For headings, buttons, and accents.
  • Accent Color: #F5A623 (Orange) – For highlights, links, and call-to-action areas.
  • Background: #F9FAFB (Light Gray) – For page background.

Typography

  • Montserrat – For headings and navigation.
  • Open Sans – For body text and paragraphs.
